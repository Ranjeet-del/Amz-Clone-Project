    At Amz-Clone, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect the following types of information when you interact with our platform:

    • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment information provided during account registration or checkout.
    • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as the pages you view, your IP address, browser type, and time spent on our site.
    • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience and track website activity.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your personal data for the following purposes:

    • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, and communicate with you about your purchases.
    • Customer Service: To provide support, handle inquiries, and resolve issues related to your account or orders.
    • Personalization: To offer personalized recommendations, promotions, and advertisements based on your preferences.
    • Site Improvements: To improve our website's performance, functionality, and user experience.
    • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

    3. Sharing Your Information

    We may share your information with third parties under the following circumstances:

    • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party companies that provide services such as payment processing, order fulfillment, and website analytics.
    • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or respond to legal processes.
    • Business Transfers: If Amz-Clone undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

    4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to track your interactions with our website and enhance your experience. You can control the use of cookies by adjusting your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

    5. Data Security

    We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

    6. Your Privacy Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
    • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications.
    • Request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.

    To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@amz-clone.com.
