    Welcome to the Amz-Clone Help Center. Here you will find answers to frequently asked questions and ways to get in touch with our support team.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    1. How do I track my order?

    You can track your order by logging into your account, going to the "Orders" section, and selecting the specific order. You will find a tracking link provided there.

    2. How do I return an item?

    If you are not satisfied with a product, you can return it within 30 days of delivery. Go to the "Orders" section, choose the item you wish to return, and follow the return instructions.

    3. What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Amazon Pay. Cash on delivery is also available in selected regions.

    4. How do I cancel my order?

    Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. Go to the "Orders" section, select the order, and if it is still eligible for cancellation, click the "Cancel" button.

    5. How can I contact customer support?

    You can contact our customer support team via email, live chat, or phone. Details are provided below.
